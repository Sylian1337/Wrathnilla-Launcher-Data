**2.1 NEW ENCOUNTER TYPE - MURDERBOTS**

**Overview**
Murderbots are merciless killers that will unexpectedly engage you if certain criteria (your character's level and current in-game location) are met. They differ from normal encounters because they will **ignore your pet tank and focus completely on killing you.**

Once your character out-levels the Murderbot, the attacks will stop, only to resume once again when you reach level 60 and begin to face their own level 60 versions.
