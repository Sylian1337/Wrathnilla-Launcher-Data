**HOW TO OBTAIN YOUR COMPANION / MINION / PET**

Companions are an integral part of Wrathnilla - you will not be able to do dungeon content or harder/elite overworld content without them.

To obtain your companion, simply play the game as you normally would. Once you reach level 10, you will receive an auto-complete quest in your race's level 10 area (so Bloodhoof Village for Tauren, Goldshire for Humans, etc).
You will gain a summoning item and a heal-over-time spell which can only be used on the companion.

Most traditionally petless classes obtain them in the area's inn. Druids, Mages and Shamans receive them from their class trainers - likewise the ones in their respective level 10 areas.

I **highly** recommend using the NeedToKnow addon to time your companion's heal-over-time ability.

Another thing I would like to point out is that, once you enter more difficult dungeons, companion management will become extremely important. Set up follow, attack and stand still keybinds for your companion and get used to using them.
