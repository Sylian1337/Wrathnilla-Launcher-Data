**WHY, WHAT AND HOW**

The purpose of this script is to make the overworld leveling experience a little more interesting. Every 1 to 3 minutes, the script will activate and aggro one enemy within 45 yards and line of sight. The aggroed enemy can pull additional enemies based on proximity. If you play with a friends, any one of you can become a victim of the script.

If you decide to play with a pet, things change slightly. The script will only trigger 70% of the time, but will aggro up to 3 enemies in range.

If you wish to toggle it off or on, use the chat command **.danger**

If you wish to temporarily disable it, type **/afk** and the script will reactivate once your away status changes.

The script is completely inactive in dungeon environments.
