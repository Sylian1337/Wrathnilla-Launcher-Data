Hello. I have been working on this repack for years at this point, and I feel that I have created a somewhat unique experience for the solo-oriented Vanilla enthusiast that does not involve bots or nerfed content. If that sounds interesting to you, please read on.

As far as the ingame world goes, I’ve always been an enormous fan of Vanilla. Later content seemed like too much of an amusement park. However, those expanded talent trees, spells and so on are too good to pass up on. I decided to mod an experience that, as I see it, combines what I feel is best about the early life stage of WoW.

While the repack is solo-oriented, I've included a script that will scale NPCs up depending on the amount of players logged in - up to a 5 player maximum.

Special thanks to Sylian for everything he's done to help me make a new Wrathnilla, to Brimstone for getting me into modding, and to the missus for putting up my obsessive modding effort.

**WHAT THIS REPACK OFFERS:**

- A thorougly overhauled and curated Vanilla dungeon experience (currently up to level 50) for the solo player - no autobalance or solocraft, everything has been done by hand

- Extreme class, talent and spell changes which will allow you much more freedom in deciding how you want to play your character (Holy Priest dps, melee Hunter, etc)

- Just plug and play - no difficulty tweaking or pre-play setup of any kind needed

- A free experience through and through - donations are very welcome but are not required, everything is available for free

- Content restored to pre Burning Crusade status - for example, abominations once again guard Undercity

- Many edits to professions, from lowering mat requirements to enhancing existing items and adding new ones to help players tackle PvE

- Customized AHbot (only sells vanilla-appropriate items)

- Several optional lua scripts to enhance your gameplay experience

note: if you are interested in doing raid content immediately, you can check out the legacy version of Wrathnilla available in my discord and other places. In my opinion it is a far inferior version gameplay-wise, but it offers all Vanilla content, fully soloable, including a new "outdoor raid".

**HOW CORE GAMEPLAY DIFFERS FROM UN-MODDED:**

The MMO trinity of healer/tank/dps has no place in a single player, duo or casual trio setting. This overhaul addresses the issue by converting all healing and tanking builds into damage dealing ones with various support and defense mechanics. The original damage-dealers have had their survivability increased considerably. Paired with a minion pet, each individual player becomes a self-contained trinity.

You will not be able to tank as before. No class is equipped to deal with continuous boss damage after their pet dies. Rely on your pet and keep it alive. To play effectively, you will need to get used to moving your pet around and keeping it's healing ability up.

You will not be able to heal as before. Those spells have had their mana cost greatly increased and most of them have long, linked cooldowns. Rely on consumables.

I cannot put enough emphasis on the consumables and crafted items. Get them and use them or you will probably fail.
