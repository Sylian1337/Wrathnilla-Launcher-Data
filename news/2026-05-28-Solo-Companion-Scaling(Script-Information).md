**WHY, WHAT AND HOW**

The purpose of this script is to spice up your solo overworld experience if you plan on leveling with a pet for company. It has been mentioned to me that playing with an active pet makes the world outside of dungeons feel too easy, and I agree.

This script will only activate if you are not in a dungeon, are playing alone (so without other players), and adventuring with a pet. 

The script will greatly increase the damage and slightly increase the health of all normal-rank NPCs. Elite and rare creatures will not be affected, and neither will Murderbots.

If you play with this and Dangerous-Overworld active at the same time, you will have to treat the overworld as a training ground for dungeons. By that I mean your pet will need healing, and you will have to tell it to swap targets during the fight. If you pull more than 3 mobs, consider crowd control. If you pull an entire camp, consider running.

The scaling deactivates on its own if the conditions aren't met. You can also manually toggle it on and off with the chat command **.scs**
