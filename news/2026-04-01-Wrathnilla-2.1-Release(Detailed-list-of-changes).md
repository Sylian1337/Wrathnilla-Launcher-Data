**2.1 DUNGEON CHANGES**

**General**
- trash and boss movesets greatly enriched or overhauled completely
- underwhelming loot tables of some bosses and minibosses improved

**Shadowfang Keep**
- Tome of Expert Resource Tracking added near Fenrus the Devourer

**Deadmines**
- Defias Watchmen and Taskmasters no longer glitch out at range

**Blackfathom Deeps**
- Baron Aquanis now remains summoned for 1 hour

**Uldaman**
- chests are now guaranteed to give one green item
- Ironaya's health slightly reduced and the duration of her Broken Bones reduced to 30 seconds

**Zul'Farrak**
- added several chests
- shallow graves now spawn fewer undead
- Sergeant Bly and Weegli Blastfuse health lowered
- added a chest with Divino-matic Rod and Weegli's Barrel (for those wishing to skip the jank of the pyramid event)

**Maraudon Purple**
- new trash: Putridus Influencer
- new boss: Moulder Deeprot
- some chests added

**Maraudon Orange**
- new trash: Foulspore Giant, Celebrian Keeper
- some ghost mushroom spawns added

**Inner Maraudon**
- Subterranean Diemetradons removed
- new trash: Deep Borer Adult, Primordial Giant, Primordial Warder, Theradrim Breaker
- new bosses: Shardfather, Skolex the Borer
- some gold vein and ghost mushroom spawns added

**Sunken Temple**
- the 6 Troll guardian minibosses are now guaranteed to drop a blue item from their shared loot table



**2.1 PET CHANGES**

**General**
- slowing effect of damage-reducing abilities reduced to 30%

**Beasts**
- pets no longer gain armor and health based on the master's equipment (equalized with Companions, damage unaffected)
**Crab:**
- Pin - renamed to Tendon Pinch, no longer channeled, no longer breakable by damage

**Demons**
- demons no longer gain armor and health based on the master's equipment (equalized with Companions, damage and mana unaffected)
**Imp:**
- Phase Shift - cooldown increased to 45 sec, duration increased to 10 sec, effect reduced to 20%
**Succubus:**
- Amorous Armor - duration set to 15 sec, effect reduced to 10%, 45 sec cooldown added
**Felhunter:**
- Devour Magic - duration reduced to 20 sec, cooldown increased to 45 sec, no longer heals, now dispels additional effects per rank, now only reduces magic damage
**Felguard:**
- Mortal Strike - redesigned into Sadistic Strike, which heals the Felguard
- Intercept - now available immediately
- New ability: Crippling Spin - attacks surrounding enemies and reduces their movement speed by 50% for 10 sec, 30 sec cooldown
**Infernal:**
- Permanent aoe fire aura removed
- New ability: Fel Inferno - deals Fire damage to enemies within 13 yards, 8 sec cooldown

**Elementals**
- new variants: Scorcher, Surger and Howler



**2.1 CLASS CHANGES**

**General**
- each class has gained an ARG-style dodge mechanic spell available from trainers at level 2

**Hunter**
- Pact of Loyalty - now learned at level 10

**Warlock**
- Felblood Infusion - now learned at level 10
- Improved Voidwalker - damage resistance effect reduced to 10% and healing effect reduced to 3/6% of total health
- Fel Domination - no longer reduces Summoning cast time, cooldown reduced to 1 min
- Demonic Empowerment - cooldown increased to 2 minutes, now also causes the Felhunter to reflect spells for 5 sec, effect duration on Succubus and Felguard reduced to 10 sec
- Improved Imp - Phase Shift duration increase set to 5/10/15 sec
- Hungry Flames - replaced with Burning Passion
- Burning Passion (new talent) - increases the duration of your Succubus' Amorous Armor by 5/10 sec and effect by 5/10%

**Priest**
- Shackle Undead - renamed to Bind Evil, now also affects demons

**Shaman**
- Earth Chant - renamed to Chant of the Elements
- Sphere of Water - new ranks added to trainer

**Druid**
- Dream Strike - new ranks added to trainer

**Paladin**
- Shield of Righteousness - now learned at level 6 (idea stolen from Brimstone)

**Rogue**
- Ghostly Strike - duration reduced to 2 sec, dodge effect increased to 100%

**Mage**
- Dragon's Breath - cooldown increased to 45 sec, duration increased to 8 sec
