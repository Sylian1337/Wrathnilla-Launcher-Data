**Overview**

Does what it says on the box. However, our version of hardcore will come with added challenges and limitations:

- you will not be able to trade with non-hardcore characters
- you will not be allowed to form a party with non-hardcore characters
- you will have to kill dungeon bosses in order to continue gaining experience

To clarify the last part with an example: once you reach level 20, **your character will stop gaining experience until you've killed** Verdant the Everliving *or* Archmage Arugal *or* Edwin VanCleef.

If this mode interests you (you masochist), visit your race's starting area graveyard. Only inexperienced level 1 characters can enable it.

Additional note: you will be allowed to disable both dangerous-overworld and murderbot scripts, those will not be a mandatory part of hardcore.
